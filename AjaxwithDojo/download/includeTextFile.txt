<h1> Overview </h1>
<b>Ajax (Asynchronous JavaScript and XML)</b> describes the
possibility to communicate with the webserver in the
backgroundand to update webpage with information from the
webserver without re-loading the webpage.

<BR> 
	
Ajax can improve the usablity of a webpage, e.g. a webpage
can quickly be displayed while information is still loaded
from the webserver.
	
<BR> 

Ajax is based on the XMLHttpRequest and is considered to by
a key technology to realise Web 2.0. For detaila on the
XMLHttpRequest please see the appendix.
